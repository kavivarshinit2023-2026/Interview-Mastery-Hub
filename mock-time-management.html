Key Tips for Strong Time Management Answers:

• Always use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)

• Show how you prioritized, planned, and adapted under pressure

• Highlight tools/techniques you used (Eisenhower, Pomodoro, time blocking, etc.)

• Emphasize results: deadlines met, productivity increased, stress reduced

• Mention learning from mistakes — shows growth mindset
