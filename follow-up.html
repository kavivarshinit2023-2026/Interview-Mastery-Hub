Follow Up

Why Following Up Matters After Interviews

A thoughtful follow-up email reinforces your interest, shows professionalism, and keeps you top-of-mind. It’s one of the easiest ways to stand out — most candidates skip this step, so doing it well can significantly boost your chances.

Practical Tips to Follow Up Effectively

  • Send within 24 hours — ideally same day or next morning after the interview.
  • Personalize it: Mention something specific from the conversation (e.g., “I enjoyed discussing the upcoming project in [team name]”).
  • Thank them: Express gratitude for their time and reiterate your excitement about the role.
  • Reaffirm fit: Briefly restate why you’re a great match (1-2 sentences).
  • Keep it short & professional: 4-6 sentences max; proofread for errors; use proper subject line like “Thank You – [Your Name] Interview for [Role]”.
Pro Tip: Send a thank you email within 24 hours — mention a specific discussion point to make it personal and memorable!
