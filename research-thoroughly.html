Research Thoroughly

Why Thorough Research Matters in Interviews

Showing that you've researched the company demonstrates genuine interest, preparation, and seriousness. It helps you tailor answers, ask smart questions, and stand out as someone who truly wants to join the team — interviewers notice this instantly!

Practical Tips to Research Effectively

  • Visit the company website: Read About Us, Products/Services, News/Blog, Careers page, and recent announcements.
  • Check LinkedIn: Follow the company page, look at recent posts, and research your interviewers' profiles (background, posts, interests).
  • Read recent news: Use Google News, company press releases, or sites like Crunchbase for funding, expansions, or challenges.
  • Understand company values & culture: Know their mission, vision, core values, and Glassdoor reviews for insider insights.
  • Prepare 2-3 smart questions: Based on your research — e.g., "How is the team adapting to recent industry changes?"
Pro Tip: Check LinkedIn profiles of interviewers — it helps build rapport and tailor your answers!
