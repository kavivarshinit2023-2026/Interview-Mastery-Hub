Build Your Professional Resume

Personal Information
Full name, professional email, phone, location, LinkedIn/GitHub profile
Pro Tip: Use a professional email (name@gmail.com) and update LinkedIn
Education
Degree, institution, year of passing, CGPA/percentage, relevant coursework
Pro Tip: List most recent degree first and include final year project
Projects
Academic projects, internships, personal work — describe objective, role & outcome
Pro Tip: Use bullet points: Problem → Your Solution → Technologies → Result
Skills
Technical skills, programming languages, tools, soft skills, certifications
Pro Tip: Match skills to job description — include keywords for ATS
Career Objective
4-6 lines summary — your career goal, key strengths & what you bring
Pro Tip: Tailor it for each job application — no generic copy-paste
Avoid These Mistakes
Spelling errors, irrelevant info, too long resume, poor formatting
Pro Tip: Keep it 1 page — clean layout, consistent font, ATS-friendly
Achievements & Activities
Hackathons, certifications, workshops, volunteering, leadership roles
Pro Tip: Quantify where possible (e.g., "Organized event for 200+ students")
