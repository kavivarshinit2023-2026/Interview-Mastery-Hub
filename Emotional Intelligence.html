Emotional Intelligence

Why it matters in interviews: Emotional intelligence demonstrates self-awareness, empathy, and the ability to manage relationships — qualities highly valued by modern employers.

Tips

  • Show self-awareness — be honest about your emotions and how they influence your work.
  • Demonstrate empathy — talk about how you understand & support colleagues' feelings.
  • Highlight self-regulation — give examples of staying calm under pressure.

Common Questions

  • How do you handle stress or pressure at work?
  • Tell me about a time you received constructive criticism.
  • Describe a situation where you had to work with a difficult team member.
  • How do you handle conflict in the workplace?
  • Give an example of when you showed empathy to a colleague or customer.

Practice these regularly with real-life examples to improve!
